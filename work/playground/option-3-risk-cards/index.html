🧮 How these numbers are calculated
▼
Days of Cover
available_units ÷ avg_daily_sales — how many days of stock remain at current run rate
Risk Classification
EXPEDITE — days_cover < lead_time (stockout before reorder arrives)
REORDER — days_cover between lead_time and 2× lead_time
HOLD — days_cover > 2× lead_time; no urgent action needed
DEADSTOCK — days_cover > 180 days (very slow / no movement)
Projected Days Short
lead_time − days_cover — estimated days you'll be out of stock before new inventory lands
Reorder Quantity
Based on 90-day forward demand coverage at current velocity, minus incoming units already ordered.
Cash Tied Up
on_hand_units × unit_cost — capital locked in slow-moving inventory
