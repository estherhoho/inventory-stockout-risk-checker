Formulas & Methodology
▾
Days of Cover
(on_hand + incoming_units - committed_units) ÷ daily_velocity
daily_velocity = units_sold_30d ÷ 30. Falls back to 90d velocity if 30d is zero.
Days Short
lead_time_days - days_of_cover
Positive means you will stockout before new stock arrives.
Reorder Quantity
daily_velocity × (lead_time + safety_buffer_days)
Safety buffer defaults to 14 days. Uses reorder_multiple if provided.
Estimated Cash Required
reorder_qty × unit_cost
Only shown when unit_cost is present in your CSV. Does not include freight or duties.
Deadstock Threshold
days_of_cover > 180 days
Flagged as HIGH if over 365 days. Off-season items are caveated, not auto-flagged for action.
Data input reminder: Velocity columns must be NET of returns. Gross sales inflate velocity and understate stockout risk. If you track returns separately, subtract them from units_sold_30d and units_sold_90d before uploading.
