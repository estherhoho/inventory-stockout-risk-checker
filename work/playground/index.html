Designer Recommendation ★ Option 5: SaaS Dashboard

Why Option 5: The Inventory Stockout Risk Checker is positioned as a recurring weekly ops tool (not a one-time calculator). The sidebar nav pattern gives each module (Stockout, Deadstock, Supplier Draft, Formulas) its own focused view without scrolling, which matches how a real weekly review session works. The dark sidebar creates a professional, credible feel appropriate for Dock to AI's business-user positioning. It scales cleanly to a Dock to AI embed and positions well for future features (saved reports, multi-file runs, alerts).

Strong alternative: Option 1 (Action Digest) if the primary use case is a mobile-first founder who wants a quick "what do I do today" answer rather than a full review session. Option 1 has much lower dev complexity and is the best choice for a pure mobile-first audience.

Hybrid suggestion: Option 5's sidebar + Option 1's action-first hero section on the Overview tab. Default the Overview tab to the numbered action list from Option 1 rather than a KPI grid.
